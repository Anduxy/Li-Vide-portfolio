All In Foundation

Service

Concept + development

Type of Project

Client brief from the organisation All In Foundation. They’re a social impact tech start up and digital non-profit foundation developing a new way of fundraising to fight extreme poverty.

Description

The overarching objective in the brief was to have a top-class app that is easy to use and that attracts a high number of recurrent users. More specifically, the project has succeeded if it receives positive feedback from test users.

Me and my team came up with a plan presented in three phases: minimal viable product, near future and dream within the timeframe of tomorrow, in the coming months and in a year.

I created a concept and visualisation of the dream phase to be implemented in a year. Based on research on the target group, understanding the motivation on donation combined with social media, it became clear that community, personalisation and gamification were three cornerstones to build a concept around.

To make sure that we continuously improved to ensure that we presented a top-notch app that was user-friendly, innovative and unique, we did user testing all through the process.

End Result

A roadmap for the app development and wireframes for all the different phases and functions.

Design Process

Research > Ideation > Prototyping > User-testing > Ideation > Prototyping > User-testing > Development > Deliver
