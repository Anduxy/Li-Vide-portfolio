HOPE

Service

Concept + mock-up

Type of Project

Client brief from the company Hope. The company is a Swedish fashion brand with a passion for high-quality garments that inspire and emphasize individual style.

Description

I came up with the idea of renting a Hope piece. Together with my team we developed this idea into a subscription service. The customer rents a couple of Hope pieces for a limited period of time and then is able to switch to other pieces for a new period. Based on a combination of several sources of quantitative data the service is especially attractive to the age range of 18-30 year olds. Catering to Hope’s value for a more sustainable fashion consumption, the solution is built with a circular economy as the core to reduce waste and have a continual use of resources.

End Result

A subscription service built with wireframes for an app.

Design Process

Research > Analysing > Ideation > Mock-up > Delivery
